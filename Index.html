Mon Guerlain Eau de Parfum

This luxurious Guerlain perfume will make you feel like a world traveler: Notes of lavender from Provence, jasmine from India, sandalwood from Australia and vanilla from Papua New Guinea define this warm, rich fragrance.

Flowerbomb Eau de Parfum

Inspired by garden parties, this fresh-smelling Viktor & Rolf fragrance is housed in a cheeky grenade-shaped bottle and earns its cult-favorite status by exploding with the super feminine, warm floral scent of jasmine, freesia and rose.

Flowerbomb Eau de Parfum

This Maison Francis Kurkdjian creation quickly rose to fragrance fame thanks to its irresistible, head-turning aroma. It's the most unique mix of jasmine, cedar and amber that’s warm, sensual and strong yet somehow soft.

Black Opium Eau de Parfum

Much like your daily cup of joe, this coffee-scented Yves Saint Laurent concoction is addictive. It blends notes of rich coffee and vanilla with orange blossom, jasmine and pear for a seductive scent that will wake you up (just in time for date night).

Coco Mademoiselle Eau De Parfum

This irresistibly sexy fragrance is a modern composition with a strong yet surprisingly fresh character. Sparks of fresh and vibrant orange immediately awaken the senses. A clear and sensual heart reveals the transparent accords.

Sì Eau de Parfum

This sophisticated and intoxicating Giorgio Armani scent is perfect for a night out, thanks to its slightly sweet, musky notes of blackcurrant nectar, rose and white cedarwood. A clear and sensual heart reveals the transparent accords.
